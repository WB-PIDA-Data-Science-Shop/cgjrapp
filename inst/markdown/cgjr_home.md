# Welcome to the **govcgjr** dashboard. 

---

## 🏛️ Project Overview

**govcgjr** is designed to provide a rigorous, data-driven framework to the institutional chapter for the country growth and jobs report. The tool facilitates comparative analysis by mapping institutional indicators against global and regional benchmarks. By focusing on "distance to the frontier" metrics, the application helps identify specific bottlenecks hindering institutional effectiveness.

## 🛠 Key Features

### **Interactive Benchmarking**
* **Evolution Over Time:** Track institutional progress using longitudinal data to see how specific metrics have shifted across years.
* **Frontier Comparison:** Compare a selected country's performance against the "frontier" (the top performers in a specific category) to visualize the gap in institutional quality.
* **Peer Group Analysis:** Contextualize data by comparing countries within the same income bracket or geographic region.

---

## 📈 Getting Started

1.  **Navigate to the 'Diagnostics' Tab:** Begin by selecting your primary country of interest.
2.  **Configure Comparisons:** Select the countries or regions you wish to use as benchmarks.
3.  **Choose Dimensions:** Use the dropdown menus to select the specific institutional indicators relevant to your analysis.
4.  **Export Results:** Download high-resolution plots and summary tables for inclusion in reports or presentations.
